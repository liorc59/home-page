# VC Design Partner Outreach — Top 20 from Seedbiz List

## Selection Criteria
- Israeli or Israel-focused (accessibility + local ecosystem)
- B2B SaaS / Software portfolio focus
- Active investors (3+ deals in last 2 years)
- Seed/early-stage focus (relevant for design partner stage)
- Portfolio likely includes companies with PMM/PM teams that need CI

---

## Outreach Strategy

**The ask:** Not investment. We're asking VCs to connect us with PMM/PM teams in their portfolio companies as design partners — free access in exchange for feedback.

**Why VCs care:** CI is a force multiplier for their portfolio. Better competitive intel = better positioning = faster growth = better returns. Offering their portfolio companies free CI tooling is a value-add they can provide.
